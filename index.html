Carbon Footprint

What is a Carbon Footprint?

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases (GHG) that are generated by your activities. These gases, primarily carbon dioxide (CO2), trap heat in the atmosphere, causing global warming. Everything you do, from driving a car to using electricity at home, contributes to your carbon footprint.

By understanding your carbon footprint, you can take steps to reduce it. This helps combat climate change and protects our planet's health.
